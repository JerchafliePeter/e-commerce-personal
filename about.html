About EasyTrader

EasyTrader is simple. Users can easily list the cards that they have and wish to sell or trade. Users may also list cards that they are looking for and the price that they are willing to pay. Users can also contact each other directly to card(s) for card(s). EasyTrader offers this flexible model in which users can buy cards using money, or trading via their own cards. The EasyTrader app allows the users to browse and buy cards just like the website. But more importantly, they can take pictures and scan their cards for easy listing of cards they wish to sell.
