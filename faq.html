What fees are there when making a purchase?
When users purchase cards using EasyTraders, 1% of the transaction goes to EasyTraders, and the rest goes to the seller.
What fees are there when I cash out?
USD is earned from selling on EasyTraders and is securly stored on your account. If you choose to cash-out, it will incur an 8% transaction fee
How do we actually trade, sell, and purchase?
Once a trade, or sale has been confirmed by both users, the seller (or both traders) will mail the cards to their recipiants. Users are responsible for stamps, shipping, etc.
What happens if I don't get my cards?
Recipiants should expect to wait approximately two weeks before filing a complaint. If the seller(or trader) is unreceptive, then EasyTraders' conflict resolution team will investigate the situation, and issue and refunds that may be neccessary.
